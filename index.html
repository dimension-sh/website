WELCOME

dimension.sh is a small public linux shell host (or pubnix system) that is open to anyone who wants to learn, experiment, and socialize with other like minded people.

We take the name from dimension.spodnet.uk.com, which was a collection of mixed Unix hosts ran at a UK college from 1997 to 2004. fifteen years after the shutdown of the system we decided to start again, with newer updated Linux and access to cheap resources.
