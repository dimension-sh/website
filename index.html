WELCOME

dimension.sh is a small linux shell host trying to carry on the tradition of the original dimension.spodnet.uk.com, which was a collection of mixed Unix hosts ran at a UK college from 1997 to 2004. Over a decade after the loss of the system one of the members decided to start again, and lose themselves in the nostalgia of the early days online.

Our aim is to allow people access to that time where everything was command line based and easier, in the loosest sense. If you'd be interested in joining our community then you can read howto on the JOIN page.
