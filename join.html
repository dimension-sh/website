JOIN

Dimension is open to anyone who wants to be invloved in our community, all we ask is that you follow the three basic RULES. If you accept them then joining is easy.

All you'll need to provide is the following bits of information:

  • The username you wish to use.
  • A SSH public key.
  • Why do you want to join the community.

Once you are ready, drop a mail to signup@dimension.sh and we'll get to you as soon as we can.
